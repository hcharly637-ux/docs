PRO endpoints provide access to advanced historical data, daily network analytics, and enriched on-chain metadata. They are available to users on the **Standard plan and above** — they are not accessible on the Free or Lite tiers. See the [rate limits](/resources/rate-limits) page for a full comparison of plan tiers.
