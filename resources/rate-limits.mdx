Every Etherscan API key operates within the rate limits of its assigned plan tier. Exceeding your plan's limit will return a `Max rate limit reached` error. Choose the tier that matches your application's throughput and data needs.

## Rate Limit Tiers

| API Tier         | Rate Limit                                          | API PRO       |
| ---------------- | --------------------------------------------------- | ------------- |
| Free             | 3 calls/second, up to 100,000 calls/day, [selected chains](/supported-chains) only | Not Available |
| Lite             | 5 calls/second, up to 100,000 calls/day             | Not Available |
| Standard         | 10 calls/second, up to 200,000 calls/day            | Available     |
| Advanced         | 20 calls/second, up to 500,000 calls/day            | Available     |
| Professional     | 30 calls/second, up to 1,000,000 calls/day          | Available     |
| Pro Plus         | 30 calls/second, up to 1,500,000 calls/day          | Available     |
| Dedicated/Custom | [Contact us](mailto:apisupport@etherscan.io)        | Available     |

<Note>
  **PRO endpoints** — including historical balances, daily network statistics, and gas analytics — require the **Standard tier or above**. They are not available on Free or Lite plans. See the full list of [PRO endpoints](/resources/pro-endpoints).
</Note>

## Upgrading Your Plan

To upgrade your API plan or explore available tiers, visit [etherscan.io/apis](https://etherscan.io/apis). For dedicated or custom rate limit arrangements, contact [apisupport@etherscan.io](mailto:apisupport@etherscan.io).
